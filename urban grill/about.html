About Urban Grill

RFMC - Urban Grill is located on 320 E. 9th Street in downtown Charlotte, NC. We set out to choose a desireable location that would offer our guests and investors a magnificent view of the surroundings and skyline of the city. Here at the Urban Grill, we are dedicated to providing a memorable experience to each and every individual that may stumble accross our website, or walk through our doors.

All of our guests will soon realize the plethora of entertainment possibilites in the Charlotte area. Endulge in anything from tailgating along with Carolina Panthers fans, enjoy a Charlotte Knights baseball inning, catch the first half of the Hornets playing a the Time Warner Cable Arena, or even book tickets for your favorite musicians coming into town directly from our website!

We understand the importance of options and making good decisions; therefore, we have partnered with TicketMaster and USDA to provide endless entertainment options, and for the folks who like to remain conscious of their personal diets while enjoying a delicious meal, you can now acquire the nutritional information for every menu item from the website!

We offer the best service around, come join us! Great food. Great people. Providing you with the experience of a lifetime, our mission is to fulfill your every need and send you on your way with a new sublime state of mind!
